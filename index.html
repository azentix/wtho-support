What is WTHO?
WTHO (Who To Hang Out) is a social networking app that helps you find people to hang out with in your area. Create events, join activities, and connect with like-minded people nearby.
How do I create an event?
To create an event:
  1. Tap the "+" button on the main screen
  2. Fill in event details (title, location, time, description)
  3. Choose who you're looking for (age range, gender preferences)
  4. Add photos if desired
  5. Tap "Create Event"
Note: You can only create one event per day.
How do I find events near me?
Events near you automatically appear on the map when you open the app. You can:
  • Browse events on the interactive map
  • Use filters to find specific types of activities
  • Search for events by location or activity type
How do I add friends?
To add friends:
  1. Go to the Friends tab
  2. Use the search function to find people by nickname
  3. Send friend requests to people you'd like to connect with
  4. Accept incoming friend requests from others
Is my location data safe?
Yes, we take your privacy seriously. Your exact location is never shared with other users. We only show your approximate area to help you find nearby events and people. You can control your privacy settings in the app.
How do I report inappropriate behavior?
If you encounter inappropriate behavior:
  1. Use the report function in the user's profile
  2. Block the user if necessary
  3. Contact our support team at whotohangout@gmail.com
We take all reports seriously and will investigate promptly.
Can I delete my account?
Yes, you can delete your account at any time:
  1. Go to Settings in the app
  2. Tap "Delete Account"
  3. Confirm your decision
This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
